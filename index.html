useful tips

Water tips

    Clean the water once in 2 weeks .

    Check the Water salinity

    Check the Water temperature

    No salty water

Fishtank Tips

    To avoid sickie fishies, never use soaps or chemicals to clean anything. .

    Give everything that goes into the tank a good rinse in normal tap water, beforehand.

    Ask our resident fish petspert if you have any questions swimming around in your head.

    Add to your finny family slowly – don’t add more than a couple of fish each week, when starting out. (This depends on your tank size too, of course.)
